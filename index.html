Imagine that you're Patrick with a clone gun that wants to make a Krabby Patty.
With your low intellect, you try to recreate the Krabby Patty through experiments
The first thing you have to do is place the back bread on the table, but you're Patrick. So you placed ketchup instead
You thought it's the right choice, so you get a container and cloned the ketchup inside it.
But then, you realize that it was wrong and you replaced the ketchup with a panda.
You thought it's the right choice, so you get another container, placed it on top of the other container from before and cloned the panda on the second container.
You basically, do the same step until you are satisfied. But you're Patrick, so you never are finished.
Now here comes Larry, and he wants to make a Krabby Patty with you. So he borrows your clone gun clones the latest container to his table. And you guys do the same steps all over again.
